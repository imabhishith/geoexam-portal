General Instructions

  1. The clock will be set at the server. The countdown timer in the top right corner of the screen will display the remaining time available for you to complete the examination. When the timer reaches zero, the examination will end automatically — no action is required from you.
  2. The Question Palette displayed on the right side of the screen will show the status of each question using one of the following symbols. The question(s) "Answered and Marked for Review" will also be considered for evaluation.
1
Not Visited — You have not visited the question yet.
2
Not Answered — You have visited but not answered.
3
Answered — You have answered the question.
4
Marked for Review — Marked for review but NOT answered. Will NOT be evaluated.
5
Answered & Marked for Review — Answered and also marked for review. Will be evaluated.

Navigating to a Question

  1. Click on the question number in the Question Palette at the right of your screen to go to that numbered question directly. Note that using this option does NOT save your answer for the current question.
  2. Click on Save & Next to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.
  3. Click on Mark for Review & Next to save your answer for the current question and also mark it for review, then move to the next question.
  4. Clicking Save & Next on the last question in the section will take you to the first question of the next section (if any).
  5. You can shuffle between sections and questions anytime during the examination as per your convenience.
  6. The candidate can click on the arrow which apperas to the left of question palette to collapse the question palette thereby maximizing the question window. To view the question palette again, you can click on arrow which appears on the right side of question window.

Answering a Question

  1. Single Correct (MCQ/SCQ): Choose one answer from the four options (A, B, C, D) given below each question. Click on the circle button of the chosen option to select your answer. To deselect your chosen answer, click on the button of the chosen option again, or click the Clear Response button.
  2. Multiple Select Questions (MSQ): Choose one or more correct answer(s) from the four options (A, B, C, D) given below each question. Click on the checkbox(es) of the chosen option(s) to select your answer(s). To deselect, click the checkbox again or use Clear Response.
  3. Numerical Answer Type (NAT): Enter the answer using the virtual numeric keypad. No options will be shown for these questions. There is no negative marking for NAT questions.
  4. To change your chosen answer for a question, click on the new answer option you wish to select — the previously chosen option will be deselected automatically.
  5. Click the Clear Response button to clear your answer and return the question to the "Not Answered" state.

Marking Scheme

  1. For questions carrying 1 mark: +1 for correct answer, −1/3 for wrong answer.
  2. For questions carrying 2 marks: +2 for correct answer, −2/3 for wrong answer.
  3. For Multiple Select Questions (MSQ): Full marks if all correct options and no wrong options are selected. Partial marks proportional to the fraction of correct options selected (only if NO wrong option is chosen). Full negative marks if any wrong option is selected.
  4. For Numerical Answer Type (NAT): Full marks if the answer lies within the specified tolerance range. No negative marking.
  5. Questions not answered will result in zero marks.

Important Points to Note

  1. Do not close or refresh the browser window during the exam. Your progress is saved automatically every 15 seconds. Closing the tab may cause loss of recent answers.
  2. Only one login session is allowed per candidate at any given time. Attempting to login from multiple devices/browsers simultaneously will result in session termination.
  3. The use of log table booklets, any written material, electronic devices, mobile phones, or scientific calculators outside the on-screen calculator is strictly prohibited.
  4. An on-screen scientific calculator is available during the exam — click the Calculator button in the header.
  5. Once the examination is submitted, you cannot re-attempt or modify your answers.
  6. Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the exam. A brief disconnection will not affect your saved answers, but prolonged disconnection may cause issues.
  7. The exam will auto-submit when the countdown timer reaches zero.
Any candidate found resorting to unfair means will be immediately disqualified. The examination is being monitored.
