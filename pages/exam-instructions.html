Navigating to a Question

  1. Click on the question number in the Question Palette at the right of your screen to go to that numbered question directly.
  2. Click on Save & Next to save your answer for the current question and then go to the next question.
  3. Click on Mark for Review & Next to save your answer for the current question, mark it for review, and then go to the next question.
  4. Click on Clear Response to clear the selection for the current question.

Marking Scheme

  1. Single Correct (SCQ): Full marks if correct. Negative marks for wrong answer. No marks for unattempted.
  2. Multiple Correct (MCQ): If ANY selected option is wrong full negative marks. If only correct options selected (partial or full) proportional positive marks based on coverage. Unattempted = 0.
  3. Numerical Answer Type (NAT): Full marks if answer matches. No negative marking. No partial marking.

Important Points

  1. Do not close or refresh the browser window during the exam. Your progress is saved automatically.
  2. You can change your answer by clicking on another option for MCQ questions.
  3. An on-screen scientific calculator is available during the exam.
  4. Ensure you have a stable internet connection throughout the exam.
  5. Submit the exam only when you are sure you have answered all questions.
  6. Once the exam is submitted, you cannot re-attempt it.
